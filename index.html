About us

Welcome to wah college

Wah College of health Sciences stands as a prominent institution in Pakistan, dedicated to the core principle of comprehending and enhancing livelihoods, as encapsulated in our motto. Within the realm of healthcare in Pakistan, it holds a significant position and aspires to contribute substantially to the advancement of healthcare education, bridging the gaps in research infrastructure, and implementing an Institute-led healthcare delivery system. This additional system, working alongside the current federal and provincial setups, envisions uplifting the quality of life for the people of Pakistan
